# TSMC — Financial Brief

TSMC is a Taiwan-headquartered company in the semiconductor_foundry segment of the semiconductor value chain.
It reports in TWD with fiscal year ending December. Ticker: TWSE:2330,NYSE:TSM.

## Income statement

In fiscal year 2024, TSMC reported revenue of 2.89 trillion TWD (US$91.66B).
Revenue growth versus 2023 was +33.9%.
Net income was 1.22 trillion TWD (US$38.55B).
Operating income reached 1.38 trillion TWD.
Gross margin was 56.6%, and operating margin was 48.8%.
Diluted earnings per share were 45.25 TWD.

## Balance sheet

As of fiscal year 2024, TSMC reported total assets of 6.69 trillion TWD.
Total liabilities were 2.37 trillion TWD, and total equity was 4.32 trillion TWD.
The equity ratio (total equity / total assets) was 64.6%.
