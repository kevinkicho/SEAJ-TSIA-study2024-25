# ASM International N.V. — Financial Brief

ASM International N.V. is a Netherlands-headquartered company in the wfe_equipment segment of the semiconductor value chain.
It reports in EUR with fiscal year ending December. Ticker: ENXTAM:ASM,NASDAQ:ASMIY.

## Income statement

In fiscal year 2024, ASM International N.V. reported revenue of 2.93 billion EUR (US$3.17B).
Revenue growth versus 2023 was +11.3%.
Net income was 685.7 million EUR (US$740.6M).
Gross margin was 50.5%, and operating margin was 27.4%.
Across the 2020–2024 window, revenue compounded at a CAGR of 21.9%.

## Balance sheet

As of fiscal year 2024, ASM International N.V. reported total assets of 4.01 billion EUR.
Total liabilities were 266.7 million EUR, and total equity was 3.75 billion EUR.
The equity ratio (total equity / total assets) was 93.4%.
